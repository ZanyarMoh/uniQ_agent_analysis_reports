Platform Overview

B14g is a Bitcoin Merge Staking platform built on Core DAO that unlocks the full potential of BTC holdings. The platform integrates Bitcoin's unparalleled economic strength with protocol-native tokens, creating a sustainable staking environment that benefits both users and protocols.

Key Principles

  • Security & Sustainability: Leverages Bitcoin's deep liquidity and robust security while allowing protocols to secure their networks without over-inflating their native token supply.
  • Win-Win Model: BTC stakers earn additional rewards while protocols benefit from enhanced security and balanced token economics.
  • Lowered Barriers: The Merge Marketplace enables collaboration between BTC holders and native token stakers, unlocking higher yield tiers and driving ecosystem growth.
