Blend Protocol on Mezo Testnet: Analytics Report

Report Date: April 30, 2025

Executive Summary

Blend Protocol has demonstrated exceptional growth since its launch on the Mezo testnet approximately one month ago. With 13,020 total users and 58,584 total transactions, the platform has shown remarkable adoption metrics, particularly in the past week with a 40.46% increase in users and 46.16% increase in transactions. This report analyzes user activity, transaction patterns, and engagement metrics while correlating them with public announcements from Blend Protocol.

2. User Growth Analysis

2.1 Overall Growth Trajectory

The user base has grown from a single user on March 30th to 13,020 by April 30th, 2025, with particularly strong growth beginning on April 8th:

  • First Week (March 30-April 7): Minimal activity (4 total users)
  • Second Week (April 8-14): Rapid acceleration (3,661 users by April 14)
  • Third Week (April 15-21): Continued strong growth (7,682 users by April 21)
  • Fourth Week (April 22-30): Maintained momentum (13,020 users by April 30)

2.2 New User Acquisition

The platform has maintained strong new user acquisition throughout April:

5. Key Insights

Strong Adoption Metrics

The 40.46% weekly user growth and 46.16% transaction growth indicate exceptional platform momentum.

Incomplete Lending Cycles

While 96%+ of users engage in Supply and Borrow actions, only ~6% complete Repay actions and ~3% perform Withdrawals, suggesting most loans remain outstanding.

Concentrated Activity

A small group of power users drives a disproportionate share of transactions, with the top 10 users accounting for 7.5% of all activity.

New User Engagement

New users appear to engage immediately with core platform functionality, with average of 5 transactions per user.

Growth Pattern Correlation

Major spikes in user and transaction activity show consistent patterns throughout the month of April.

6. Recommendations

  1. Focus on Loan Completion: Implement strategies to encourage users to complete the full lending cycle through Repay and Withdraw actions.
  2. Power User Program: Develop a formalized program to recognize and incentivize power users who drive significant platform activity.
  3. User Retention: As new user acquisition shows occasional volatility, implement retention strategies to maintain consistent daily active user metrics.
  4. Diversification of Activity: Encourage broader participation beyond the top users to reduce concentration risk.
  5. Marketing Alignment: Continue synchronizing marketing initiatives with platform metrics to capitalize on growth momentum.

This report is based on Blend Protocol activity on the Mezo testnet from March 30 to April 30, 2025.
