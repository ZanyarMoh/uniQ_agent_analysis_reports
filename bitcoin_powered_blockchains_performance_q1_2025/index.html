Key Findings and Insights

Market Leadership

Core emerges as the clear leader across most metrics, particularly in user adoption where it captured over 11 million new addresses among the Bitcoin-powered blockchains analyzed. Its TVL leadership ($531.6M) and transaction volume superiority (36.28M transactions) further cement its position.

Growth Dynamics

While Core leads in absolute terms, BÂ² Network demonstrated superior efficiency metrics with high transactions per user and competitive TVL per transaction. BounceBit showed interesting adoption patterns with explosive user growth in March despite maintaining the smallest TVL.

TVL Trends

The overall ecosystem of these Bitcoin-powered blockchains experienced TVL contraction in Q1 2025, with six of seven chains showing TVL decreases from January to March. Only BOB bucked this trend with a 12.5% TVL increase. The combined ecosystem saw TVL decrease from approximately $2.19B to $1.68B (-23.3%).

Efficiency Leaders

Different chains excel in different efficiency metrics:

  • Transaction Efficiency: BounceBit (1,143 daily transactions per $1M TVL)
  • User Engagement: BÂ² Network (2,975 transactions per user)
  • Cost Efficiency: BounceBit ($9.72 TVL per transaction)
  • Value Concentration: Rootstock ($86,103 TVL per user)

Conclusions and Future Outlook

The Bitcoin-powered blockchain ecosystem demonstrated significant diversity in Q1 2025, with different platforms focusing on various aspects of performance and user experience. While Core clearly dominates in volume metrics, smaller platforms show competitive advantages in efficiency and specific use cases.

The overall decrease in TVL across most platforms suggests either a broader market correction or migration to platforms outside this analysis. However, the strong user growth in Core and the late surge in BounceBit adoption indicate continued interest in Bitcoin-powered blockchain solutions despite the TVL decline.

Looking ahead, networks that can balance efficiency with user acquisition and retention are likely to gain advantages.
